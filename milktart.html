Milk tart is a traditional South African dessert consisting of a sweet pastry crust filled with a creamy, custard-like mixture made from milk, sugar, flour, and eggs, often flavored with cinnamon. The dessert has Dutch and Cape Malay origins, and is a beloved treat enjoyed for its rich, comforting flavors and silky texture.
